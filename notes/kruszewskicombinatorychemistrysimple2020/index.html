Combinatory Chemistry: Towards a Simple Model of Emergent Evolution by Kruszewski, G., & Mikolov, T. (2020)

tags
Artificial life, Combinatory logic
source
(Kruszewski, Mikolov 2020)

Summary

This is Kruszewski’s approach to artificial life, based on artificial chemistry. Combinatory logic is used as a basis for this system. Conservation laws are added on top of the set of rules that make combinatory logic Turing complete. This is then used to observe interesting dynamics and pre-life-like processes.
