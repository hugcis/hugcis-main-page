Konrad Zuse

resources
Juergen Schmidhuber’s page

In 1941, he constructed the first fully functional programmable computer, the Z3.

He suggested in 1967 in his book Calculating space that the universe is running on a Cellular automaton. This is now known as Zuse’s thesis.
