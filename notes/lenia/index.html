Lenia

tags
Cellular automata
papers
(Chan 2019, 2020)

Lenia is a continuous cellular automaton. It is sometimes referred to as a “continuous Conway’s Game of Life”.

Bibliography

Chan, Bert Wang-Chak. May 2019. “Lenia - Biology of Artificial Life”. arXiv:1812.05433 [Nlin], May.

———. May 2020. “Lenia and Expanded Universe”. arXiv:2005.03742 [Nlin], May.
