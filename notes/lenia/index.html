Lenia

tags
Cellular automata
papers
(Chan 2019; Chan 2020)

Lenia is a continuous cellular automaton. It is sometimes referred to as a “continuous Conway’s Game of Life”.

Bibliography

  1. Bert Wang-Chak Chan. May 4, 2019. "Lenia - Biology of Artificial Life". Arxiv:1812.05433 [nlin]. http://arxiv.org/abs/1812.05433.
  2. Bert Wang-Chak Chan. May 7, 2020. "Lenia and Expanded Universe". Arxiv:2005.03742 [nlin]. http://arxiv.org/abs/2005.03742.
