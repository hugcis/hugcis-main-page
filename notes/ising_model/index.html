Simulation of an Ising model by a cellular automaton

Several works have proposed and later refined cellular automaton-base algorithms of Ising models. (Vichniac 1984; Herrmann 1986; Ottavi, Parodi 1989)

Bibliography

  1. Gérard Y. Vichniac. January 1, 1984. "Simulating Physics with Cellular Automata". Physica D: Nonlinear Phenomena 10 (1):96–116. DOI.
  2. H. J. Herrmann. October 1, 1986. "Fast Algorithm for the Simulation of Ising Models". Journal of Statistical Physics 45 (1):145–51. DOI.
  3. H. Ottavi, O. Parodi. April 1989. "Simulation of the Ising Model by Cellular Automata". Europhysics Letters (EPL) 8 (8). IOP Publishing:741–46. DOI.
