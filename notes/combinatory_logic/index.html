It was independently invented by Moses Schönfinkel, John Von Neumann and Haskell Curry.

A Turing-complete basis of operators is:

  • \(If\quad\triangleright\quad f\)
  • \(Kfg \quad\triangleright\quad f\)
  • \(Sfgx \quad\triangleright\quad fx(gx)\)
